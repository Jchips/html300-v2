iPhone info

Features:

  • Siri
  • Airdrop
  • Apple Pay
  • True tone display
  • Tap-to-top
  • Dual stereo speakers
  • Wireless & fast charging
  • iMessage & FaceTime
  • Silent & ring physical switch
  • Animoji/Memoji [exception: SE (2022)]
  • 3D Face unlock [exception: SE (2022)]
  • IP68 water/dust resistant [exception: SE (2022)]

Camera:

  • 4K video @ 60fps on all cameras
  • Night mode
  • Ultrawide camera

Pros:

  • Best app optimization on a smartphone
  • Best at running games
  • Considered the smoothest running phones/really good software
  • Free earpods ITB
  • Apple Watch is the best smartwatch on the market
  • Updates phones for at least 5yrs

Cons:

  • Very limited customization
  • No headphone jack
  • Face ID doesn't work when phone is flat on table?
  • Doesn't use common charger (USB Type C)
  • Large notch [exceptions: SE (2020), 8 Plus and older]
  • Transferring files between computer and phone is a bit complicated because of Apple being proprietary
  • Google Assistant and Google Maps are better than Siri and Apple Maps
  • Slowest charging in the industry
  • Two-step face unlock
  • Camera protrudes
